About Word Counter

freewordcounter.io is a free online word count and text analysis tool designed to help users quickly and accurately perform calculations right in their browser.

Our Mission

We believe useful tools should be free, fast, and accessible to everyone. Our word count and text analysis tool requires no downloads, no sign-ups, and no installations — just open the page and start using it.

How It Works

Our tool runs entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. This means your data never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy. There's nothing to install and it works on any modern browser.

Features

  • 100% free with no hidden costs
  • No registration or sign-up required
  • Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • All processing happens in your browser
  • No data is collected or stored
  • Fast and accurate results
